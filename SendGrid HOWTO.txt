1. Create a free DNS domain on forexample noip.com
2. Add a MX record to your DNS domain. Enter "mx.sendgrid.net" into the MX record.
3. Register an account on Sendgrid.net.
4. Goto https://sendgrid.com/developer/reply
5. In the "hostname" input box enter the name of your domain that you created in step 1.
6. In the "url" input box enter the url path to your server, where you want to receive your emails (if you are using this server then you should enter "http://my.domain.name.com/inbound").
7. Create a new Google calendar account and enter the domain that you created in step 1 as your email address.
8. Add event notifications to your calendar
